The Process

Three steps to ensure continuity.

We remove traditional administrative friction. VaultAfter operates beautifully on the surface, backed by exceptional architectural resilience underneath.

  1. Secure Your Assets

    Organize your keys, documents, and credentials in your personal secure dashboard. Everything is isolated immediately.

  2. Define Your Safeguards

    Set a personalized check-in routine and name your chosen beneficiaries. The system maintains watch silently behind the scenes.

  3. Seamless Handover

    If you stop checking in and your custom grace period closes, your secure vault is safely and gracefully unlocked for your designated heirs.
